Grandma's Chicken

Description

Grandma Marijo's delicious grilled chicken.

Ingridients

  • Wild leek.
  • Onion.
  • Chives.
  • Bouillon cube.
  • Garlick powder.
  • Soy sauce.
  • Butter.
  • Olive oil.
  • Chicken breasts.

Steps

  1. Cut onion, wild leek, and chives into really small bits.
  2. Cut the chicken to desired size and remove fat.
  3. Throw onion, wild leek, chives, garlick powder, soy sauce, bouillon cube, butter, and olive oil into a pan.
  4. Mix well into a sauce.
  5. Cook the sauce on high until it looks golden.
  6. Move the onion, wild leek, and chive bits out of the sauce and into a tupperware, but leave the oil from the sauce behind. You'll use that to grill the chicken.
  7. Grill the chicken on medium.
  8. After the chicken is cooked, add the chives, wild leek, and onion bits back into the pan.
  9. Cover the pan and leave for a few minutes with the stove off.
  10. All done!
